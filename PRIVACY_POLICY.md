## 1. Information We Collect

We only collect data necessary to provide our services:

- **Account Information:**  
  When you log in using Google, we receive your email and basic profile details via Supabase.

- **Submission Data:**  
  Answers you type or PDF files you upload are processed to generate feedback and scores.

- **Usage Data:**  
  Basic anonymous data (e.g., feature usage) to improve performance and user experience.

Your data is used to:
